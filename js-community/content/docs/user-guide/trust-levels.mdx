# Trust Levels

JS Community uses a **5-tier trust level system** modeled on Discourse. Trust levels represent how invested a member is in the community and gate progressively broader permissions. Promotions happen automatically in the background — you do not need to apply.

## Trust level overview

| Level | Name | How you get it |
|-------|------|---------------|
| **0** | New User | Default at registration |
| **1** | Basic | Meet participation minimums |
| **2** | Member | Consistent, active participation |
| **3** | Regular | Long-term, high-quality contribution |
| **4** | Leader | Manually granted by admins |

## Promotion requirements

### Level 1 — Basic

You must meet **all** of the following:

- Visited the forum on at least **1 day**
- Entered (opened) at least **5 topics**
- Read at least **30 posts**
- Spent at least **10 minutes** reading
- Given at least **1 like**

### Level 2 — Member

You must meet **all** of the following:

- Visited on at least **15 days**
- Entered at least **20 topics**
- Read at least **100 posts**
- Spent at least **1 hour** reading
- Received at least **1 like**
- Given at least **10 likes**
- Created at least **3 topics**
- Made at least **10 posts**

### Level 3 — Regular

You must meet **all** of the following:

- Visited on at least **50 days**
- Entered at least **100 topics**
- Read at least **500 posts**
- Spent at least **4 hours** reading
- Received at least **20 likes**
- Given at least **50 likes**
- Created at least **10 topics**
- Made at least **50 posts**

### Level 4 — Leader

Granted manually by an admin. There are no automatic requirements.

## Permissions by trust level

| Permission | TL 0 | TL 1 | TL 2 | TL 3 | TL 4 |
|-----------|------|------|------|------|------|
| Read all public topics | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Create topics | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Reply to posts | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Like posts | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Send private messages (daily limit) | 10/day | 20/day | 50/day | 100/day | Unlimited |
| Upload images | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Create new tags | — | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Recategorize topics | — | — | — | ✓ | ✓ |
| Close/rename topics | — | — | — | — | ✓ |
| Moderate (like staff) | — | — | — | — | ✓ |

## Rate limits by trust level

| Action | TL 0 | TL 1 | TL 2 | TL 3 | TL 4 |
|--------|------|------|------|------|------|
| Topics per day | 3 | 5 | 10 | 20 | Unlimited |
| Replies per day | 10 | 20 | 50 | 100 | Unlimited |
| Likes per day | 20 | 40 | 75 | 150 | Unlimited |

## How promotions run

Trust level promotion checks run as a background job. The Inngest `cleanup-rate-limits` and trust level service evaluate each active user's stats against the requirements and promote them if they qualify. This means promotion may not be instant — typically within an hour of meeting requirements.

Admins can also manually set a user's trust level from the `/admin/users` panel.

## Demotion

Trust levels can be manually demoted by admins. There is no automatic demotion for inactivity, but admins can lower a user's trust level as part of a moderation action.
