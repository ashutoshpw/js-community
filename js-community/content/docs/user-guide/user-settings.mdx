# User Settings

Your account settings are spread across two areas: the **public profile** (what others see) and **account preferences** (private configuration).

## Public profile

Edit your public profile at `/forum/u/[username]/edit`.

### Fields

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Display name** | Shown next to your avatar; separate from your username |
| **Bio** | Short description visible on your profile page |
| **Location** | Optional; shown on your profile (e.g., "Berlin, Germany") |
| **Website** | Optional URL — link to your portfolio, GitHub, etc. |

### Avatar

Upload a profile avatar from the edit page. Requirements:

- Formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, or WebP
- Maximum size: **5 MB**
- Stored on Vercel Blob; served via CDN

If no avatar is uploaded, a default placeholder is displayed.

## Username

To change your username:

1. Go to your profile edit page
2. Update the **Username** field
3. Save changes

Constraints:
- Must be unique across the entire platform
- The check-username API (`GET /api/users/check-username?username=...`) lets you verify availability before saving

Note: Old profile URLs using your previous username will not redirect automatically.

## Password

To change your password:

1. Go to account settings
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter and confirm your new password (8–128 characters)

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. You cannot view your current password; only reset it.

## Email address

Your email address is used for:

- Account recovery (password reset)
- Transactional notifications (when email delivery is configured)

To update your email, go to account settings. Email changes may require re-verification depending on deployment configuration.

## Account deletion

To request account deletion, contact the platform team via `/contact` or use the **Delete Account** option in your account settings. Deletion is permanent and may anonymize your authored posts in line with the Privacy Policy.
