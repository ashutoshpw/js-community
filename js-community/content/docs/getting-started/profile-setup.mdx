# Profile Setup

Your profile is public and accessible at `/forum/u/[username]`. It shows your posts, activity, badges, and the information you add here.

## Accessing the profile edit page

After logging in, go to:

```
/forum/u/[username]/edit
```

Or click your avatar in the forum header and select **Edit Profile**.

## Profile fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Your display name (distinct from your username). Can include spaces and special characters. |
| **Bio** | A short description of yourself. Supports plain text. |
| **Location** | Optional city or country — shown on your public profile. |
| **Website** | Optional URL to your personal site, GitHub, or portfolio. |

## Uploading an avatar

1. On the profile edit page, click **Upload Avatar**
2. Choose a JPEG, PNG, GIF, or WebP image
3. Maximum file size is **5 MB**
4. The image is uploaded to Vercel Blob and the URL is saved to your profile
5. Your avatar appears in the forum header, next to your posts, and on your profile page

If no avatar is uploaded, a default placeholder is shown.

## Username changes

You can update your username from the profile settings. Keep in mind:

- Usernames must remain unique across the platform
- Existing links to `/forum/u/[old-username]` will not automatically redirect
- Update any external references (e.g., bio links, bookmarked URLs) after changing

## Viewing your activity

Your public profile includes two tabs accessible to all logged-in users:

- **Activity** at `/forum/u/[username]/activity` — all topics created and posts made
- **Bookmarks** at `/forum/u/[username]/bookmarks` — posts and topics you have saved privately

Your trust level is also shown on your profile. See [Trust Levels](/docs/user-guide/trust-levels) for how it is calculated.
